we aim to keep pricing simple, transparent, and consistent for everyone using the platform.

how pricing works

unlike many similar services, the price does not vary between companions. All companions on the platform follow the same standard pricing structure. the final price may depend on a few factors, such as:

  • the type of event (e.g. wedding, dinner, company event)
  • the duration of the event
  • the location of the event

you will always see the total price before confirming your booking, so there are no unexpected costs.

what the price includes

the listed price typically covers:

  • the companions time during the event
  • basic preparation and coordination beforehand
  • travel within the agreed area

if an event lasts longer than originally planned, additional hours may be added to the final price.

payment process

all payments are handled securely through the platform. when you make a booking:

  1. You select a companion and enter your event details.
  2. Payment is completed at checkout.
  3. The payment is held securely by the platform until the event has taken place.
  4. After the event, the payment is released to the companion.

this ensures a safe and reliable experience for both users and companions.

cancellations

plans can change, and we try to keep things flexible. you can cancel your booking up to 24 hours before the scheduled event and receive a full refund. cancellations made less than 24 hours before the event may not be eligible for a refund.
